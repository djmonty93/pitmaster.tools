Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data Pitmaster Tools collects, how third-party advertising and analytics services may process information, and what choices you have regarding cookies and privacy rights.

What Data We Collect

Pitmaster Tools does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information directly through the calculator itself. The calculator runs in your browser and does not ask you to submit names, phone numbers, mailing addresses, or payment information.

Third-party services used on the site, including Google Analytics and Google AdSense, may set cookies or similar technologies in your browser when enabled. Those third parties may collect device, browser, approximate location, page-view, referral, and ad-interaction data according to their own policies.

Google Analytics and Google AdSense

Pitmaster Tools may use Google Analytics to understand overall site usage and Google AdSense to serve advertising. Google and its partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to measure traffic, report on ad performance, and show ads based on your visits to this site or other websites.

Google may use collected information for ad personalization, frequency capping, fraud prevention, performance reporting, and interest-based advertising. If you consent to personalized advertising, Google may infer interests from browsing behavior across sites and services where Google advertising technologies are used.

Cookie Policy

First-party cookies are cookies set by Pitmaster Tools itself. At this time, Pitmaster Tools uses only a consent-status cookie to remember whether you accepted or rejected non-essential cookies.

Third-party cookies are cookies set by outside providers, such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense. Those cookies may be used to measure site usage, limit repeated ads, remember preferences, and support personalized or non-personalized advertising depending on your consent choices.

If you reject non-essential cookies, analytics and advertising storage remain disabled by default. Essential browser storage used to remember your consent choice may still be stored so the banner does not reappear on every page load.

Your GDPR and CCPA Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with similar privacy laws, you may have rights to request access to personal data, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, objection to certain processing, and data portability where applicable.

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, including the right to know what categories of personal information are collected, the right to request deletion of personal information held by a business, and the right to opt out of certain sharing or sale activities where applicable.

Because Pitmaster Tools itself does not maintain user accounts or directly store personal data through the calculator, many privacy requests may need to be directed to the third-party providers that place cookies or process data on the site.

How to Opt Out

You can manage Google ad personalization preferences through Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/.

You can also use the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tools: https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

You may additionally control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting existing cookies. Some privacy tools and browser extensions may also help manage interest-based advertising preferences.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests related to this site, contact: contact@pitmaster.tools.

Changes to This Policy

Pitmaster Tools may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, service providers, advertising practices, or site functionality. The current version is always the one posted on this page.
